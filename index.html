SERVICES

PC and Laptop Cleaning

Keep your devices running cool and quiet with our thorough cleaning service. We remove dust and debris from fans, vents, and components, helping to prevent overheating and extend the life of your PC or laptop.

Console Repairs (PlayStation, Xbox)

Don’t let a faulty console interrupt your gaming! We diagnose and repair common issues for PlayStation and Xbox systems, getting you back in the game quickly with reliable, expert service.

SSD and RAM Upgrades

Boost your computer’s speed and performance with our SSD and RAM upgrade services. Enjoy faster load times, smoother multitasking, and a more responsive experience—perfect for work, play, or anything in between.

Thermal Paste & Pad Replacement

Maintain optimal temperatures and performance with fresh thermal paste and pad replacement. This service helps prevent overheating, reduces fan noise, and can even extend the lifespan of your device.

Custom PC Builds

Dreaming of a custom PC? We’ll help you design and build a system tailored to your needs—whether it’s for gaming, creative work, or everyday use. Get expert advice and a machine that’s truly yours.

Full Diagnostics & Troubleshooting

Not sure what’s wrong with your device? Our comprehensive diagnostics and troubleshooting service pinpoints issues fast. We’ll explain the problem in plain language and recommend the best solution, so you’re never left in the dark.
